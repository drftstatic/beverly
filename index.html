The Great Bitter Truth

It is not for everyone. That is the entire point.

Beverly was introduced for the Italian market in 1969 as a non-alcoholic aperitif. It is clear, carbonated, bitter, citrusy, and famous in the United States mostly because people take one sip, look betrayed, and immediately offer it to someone they love.
