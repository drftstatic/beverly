Ritual Travel Plan

The three-sip method.

  1. First sip: Say nothing. The beverage has the floor.
  2. Second sip: Recalibrate. You are not drinking a candy. You are attending a tiny meeting.
  3. Third sip: Offer a friend a cup and speak softly: "It is better if you do not know."
